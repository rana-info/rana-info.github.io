About Us

History

The Ambey Private Industrial Training Institute, established in the year 2013 with the mission to produce efficient craftsmen from all over state & country and especially for those who belong to rural areas, so that they can contribute to society, industries, and can provide challenging environment for research, design, teaching & learning.
Ambey ITI has several well-equipped laboratories as per the prescribed syllabus and guidelines of N.C.V.T. The students are also exposed to computer system & virtual laboratory simulation packages like MATLAB, CAD, ORCAD PCB Designer etc. Ambey Private Industrial Training Institute, has its own library and has more than 150 books besides educational CDs on various topics of engineering.
